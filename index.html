About Me

Highly motivated, driven, and ready for opportunities as a front-end, back-end, or full-stack web developer.

I graduated from Amherst College in 2015 with a Bachelor's in Psychology. While I enjoyed my time working as a medical scribe for a psychiatrist and then going on to be a Senior Peer Support Specialist for at-risk youth, I realized I have always had a love for technology. Having taught myself how to produce music with different softwares over 12 years ago, I started figuring out how these applications were built. I grew passionate about the idea of building something that anybody anywhere could fall in love with using, as I did with those music softwares. I attended the Coding Dojo full-stack development bootcamp and received the highest scores of achievement in each stack: Python, MERN , and C# (Entity Framework Core). I am so excited to continue my journey in being a part of building things people love.
